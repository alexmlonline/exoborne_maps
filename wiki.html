Enemies

Overview of hostile factions and creatures encountered across Exoborne.

Raiders

Human adversaries that patrol key routes and loot hubs. Travel in small groups and favor mid-range weapons.

Security Drones

Autonomous aerial units guarding restricted zones. Vulnerable to EMP and precision fire.

Wildlife

Unpredictable and territorial. Avoid provoking packs; use elevation and obstacles.

Quests

Mission lines, requirements, and recommended preparation.

First Contact

  • Objective: Reach the relay station and power the uplink.
  • Tips: Bring a battery cell and basic tools; expect light resistance.

Salvager's Route

  • Objective: Recover 3 machinery parts from industrial sites.
  • Tips: Containers are often locked; plan for detours and keys.

Exo Rigs

Loadouts, modules, and playstyle notes for different rig configurations.

Scout

Lightweight mobility with scan tools. Ideal for reconnaissance and waypoint marking.

Operator

Balanced rig supporting EMP utilities and medium armor for versatile engagements.

Bulwark

Heavily armored with crowd-control options. Excels at objective defense.
